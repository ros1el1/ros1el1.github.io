Take a Brake

A website designed to encourage the working class to take a step away and to take a break. The initiative is a play on words, as individuals are reminded to slow to a brake rather than overworking themselves, causing a crash. The website is coded using HTML, CSS, and Javascript

Click HERE to play and learn more!

Trash to Treasure

A collection style game that intends to raise awareness for environmentalism by discouraging overconsumption. Targeted towards high schoolers and introduces recycling benefits through a fun and interactive game. Coded using P5.js (Javascript).

Click HERE to play the game!

Hangry Hippo

A playful twist on the childhood favorite, Hungry Hungry Hippo. Aimed towards teens who grew up with the nostalgic classic, this game has increased difficulty with moving obstacles and is complete with engaging sound effects. Coded with Javascript via P5Play.

Click HERE to visit the site!

SLHS FBLA Website

I designed the official website for the Seven Lakes High School's chapter for Future Business Leaders of America. I utilized skills and knowledge of user experience and incorporated the official colors of the organization.

Click HERE to talk to the chatbot!

COVID-19 Chatbot

A chatbot coded during the COVID-19 outbreak at its peak in order to educate individuals, specifically adolescents, on the impact of the pandemic. Designed to be interactive for increased engagement.
